Site Purpose

The Ghana National Chamber of Commerce and Industry serves the community by providing valuable resources and information for businesses and residents. Our website has two main purposes:

  1. To promote local businesses and networking opportunities for our members.
  2. To provide information on economic development, community events, and public policies that impact our area.

Scenarios

  1. What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  2. Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  3. What has been the population growth in the Carlsbad area?

Color Schema

We have selected a color palette that represents our organization's purpose and brand:

#3498DB
#d1b7b4

The primary color (#3498DB) will be used for headings, links, and primary call-to-action buttons. The secondary color (#d1b7b4) will be used for accents and secondary elements.

Typography

We will use the following fonts:

  • Heading Font: "Roboto Slab"
  • Body Font: "Open Sans"

The "Roboto Slab" font will be used for headings, and "Open Sans" will be used for the body text.
